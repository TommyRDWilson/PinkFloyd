A Momentary Lapse of Reason

7 September 1987

A Momentary Lapse of Reason is the thirteenth studio album by Pink Floyd. It was released in the UK and US on 7 September 1987 by EMI and Columbia. It was the first Pink Floyd album to not feature Roger Waters.
